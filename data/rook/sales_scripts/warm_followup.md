# Rook — Warm Follow-Up Call Script

## When to Use

Call leads who:
- Opened a Curbsite email (email tracking shows open event)
- Clicked the mockup preview link
- Visited curbsite.co from the email
- Replied to an email but didn't commit

These calls are explicitly **warm** — the prospect has already engaged.
They remembered the email. They're not surprised to hear from you.

This is NOT a cold call script. Do NOT use for prospects with no prior engagement.

---

## BEFORE THE CALL

Check the CRM dossier. Know:
- [ ] What email they opened / link they clicked
- [ ] When they opened it (today? 3 days ago?)
- [ ] Whether the mockup has been sent → URL ready
- [ ] Their business type and niche
- [ ] Their mid-tier price
- [ ] Whether founding spots are open

---

## THE SCRIPT

---

### Opening

> "Hey, is this [Owner Name]? This is Rook from Curbsite.
> I sent you an email a few days ago about [Business Name]'s website —
> looks like you got a chance to look at it. Just wanted to follow up
> and see what you thought."

*[This works because you're NOT cold. You're referencing their action.]*

---

### If They Clicked the Mockup Link

> "Did you get a chance to see the mockup I built for [Business Name]?
> I'd love to know what you thought — even if it's 'not really my style,'
> that's useful feedback."

**If they liked it:**
> "Glad to hear it. What stood out to you? [Let them answer.]
> That's exactly the direction we'd take it. So here's the next step..."

**If they didn't love it:**
> "What would you change? The mockup is just a starting point —
> the actual build is custom to your brand, your photos, your vibe.
> What would make it feel more like [Business Name]?"

**If they haven't looked yet:**
> "No worries. I'll wait — I can hold while you pull it up, or I can
> text you the link right now and you look tonight and I'll call back
> tomorrow. Which is easier?"

---

### If They Opened the Email But Didn't Click

> "I noticed you saw the email but not sure if you got a chance to
> check out the mockup. It's literally 30 seconds to look at —
> I built it specifically for [Business Name]. Want me to text you
> the link right now?"

---

### The Pivot to Close

> "Look, here's why I'm calling instead of just emailing:
> I want to make sure [Business Name] actually sees this before the
> founding client spots fill up / I move on to other businesses in [city]."

**Founding offer (if spots available):**
> "We're doing free builds for the next [N] businesses — fully custom,
> zero cost. You'd be a great fit. You interested in hearing more?"

**Standard close (no founding offer):**
> "The [mid tier name] package for a [niche] like yours runs around
> $[mid price]. 50/50 payment. Most sites are live within 2 weeks.
> Is that in the ballpark for you?"

---

### Common Responses on Follow-Up Calls

**"I was meaning to call you back"**
> "Perfect timing then. What questions do you have?"

**"I'm still thinking about it"**
> "What are you trying to figure out? Cost? Timeline? Whether it'll
> actually work for your business? I can answer any of those right now."

**"Now's not a great time financially"**
> "Totally understand. Two things: one, we do have the entry-level
> package starting at $[entry price] if budget is the main thing.
> Two, if you're one of our founding clients, it's completely free —
> I can't promise that'll still be available in a month though."

**"I talked to my spouse / partner and they're not sure"**
> "Could both of you hop on a 15-minute call with Steele, the founder?
> He's great at answering the 'is this really worth it' question —
> and he's not a pushy salesperson, just a straight-shooter."

**"I went with someone else"**
> "No problem — good for you for getting it done. Who'd you go with?
> [Listen.] If you ever need anything updated or want to switch at
> some point, we're here. Good luck with [Business Name]."

---

### Close

Same as the main closing script — pick the right track:
- Track 1: Founding offer → Calendly
- Track 2: Mockup review → move to payment
- Track 3: Just book the Steele call

---

## Timing

| Section | Time |
|---------|------|
| Opening | 20 sec |
| Mockup reference | 30 sec |
| Pivot to close | 60 sec |
| Handle response | 90 sec |
| Close | 60 sec |
| **Total** | **~4–5 min** |

## CRM Actions After the Call

| Outcome | CRM Status | Next Action |
|---------|-----------|-------------|
| Said yes | `agreed_pending` | Notify Steele (Gate 1) |
| Still thinking | `followed_up` | Set 7-day follow-up task |
| Asked a question | `review_needed` | Flag for Steele |
| Not interested | `lost` | 90-day re-engage |
| Left voicemail | `followed_up` | Send follow-up text/email |
